# CalFresh Expense & Benefits Tracker

## Description
The CalFresh Expense & Benefits Tracker is an intuitive, community-driven web application tailored to help Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP/CalFresh) recipients seamlessly organize their food budgets. Navigating monthly balances, tracking EBT-eligible purchases, and organizing grocery receipts can be incredibly stressful for busy families. This application demystifies nutritional budgeting by enabling users to categorize expenditures dynamically, calculate remaining food stamp allowances instantly, and digitalize receipts for audit protection. By transforming chaotic monthly financial tracking into a highly visual, accessible dashboard, CalFresh empowers individuals to optimize their food security, avoid unexpected transaction declines at grocery checkouts, and eliminate nutritional anxiety. Built with a commitment to clean design and structural accessibility, the platform ensures that low-income households have a reliable digital companion to maximize their food distributions efficiently, budget sustainably, and focus on what truly matters: keeping healthy, nourishing food on the kitchen table.

## Features
* **EBT Balance Tracking:** Keep an active running total of your monthly CalFresh benefit balances.
* **Eligible Categorization:** Tag expenses as EBT-Eligible or Non-Eligible automatically.
* **Digital Receipt Generator:** Generate clean, printable summaries of your food expenditures.
* **Dynamic Expense Form:** Quickly log groceries, locations, dates, and costs.
* **Fully Responsive:** Beautiful mobile-first user interface for seamless tracking directly in grocery aisles.

## Technologies Used
* **Frontend Library:** React.js (v18+)
* **Styling:** Semantic CSS3 Modules
* **Icons:** FontAwesome Custom Elements
